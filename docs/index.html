Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 March 2026

1. Overview

Kamisado ("the App") is a mobile board game developed and operated by Pear Nagar. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use the App, how it is used, and what choices you have.

By installing or using the App you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please uninstall the App.

2. Information We Collect

The App itself does not collect, transmit, or store any personally identifiable information directly. However, the third-party advertising SDK described below may collect certain data automatically.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect data subject to their own privacy policies:

Google AdMob

The App displays advertisements served by Google AdMob, a service of Google LLC. AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID), IP address, and usage data to serve ads and measure ad performance.

Ads are requested with requestNonPersonalizedAdsOnly: true, which limits personalized ad targeting. Google's data practices are governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

Google Play Services

On Android, the App is distributed via the Google Play Store and relies on Google Play Services for core platform functions. Google Play Services may collect data as described in the Google Privacy Policy.

4. Data Stored on Your Device

The App may store game settings and statistics locally on your device using Android's hardware-backed Keystore (via expo-secure-store). This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App is rated for general audiences and does not target children. Ads are configured to comply with Google's Families Policy .

6. Data Retention

Because the App does not collect personal data on our servers, there is no server-side retention to describe. Locally stored data (settings/stats) can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the App.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Opt out of personalised advertising via your device's advertising settings.
  • Reset your Android Advertising ID in Settings → Google → Ads.
  • Request information about data held by Google by visiting myaccount.google.com.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
